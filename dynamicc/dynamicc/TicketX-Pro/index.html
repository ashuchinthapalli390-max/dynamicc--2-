Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to My Bookings, click on the specific booking, and click the cancel button. Note: Cancellations are subject to terms.

Where are my tickets?

Tickets are stored in the My Bookings section and also emailed to your registered address.

What payment methods are supported?

We support Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Google Pay, Paytm, and UPI Scanner.

How do I add money to my wallet?

Go to your Profile page and click "Add Money" to top up your wallet balance.
