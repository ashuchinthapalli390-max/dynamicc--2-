🎫 Booking & Tickets

How do I book a ticket? ▼

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Select your category (Movies, Trains, Flights, or Bus)
  2. Search for your desired event/route with date and location
  3. Select available seats
  4. Review and confirm your booking
  5. Proceed to checkout
  6. Enter passenger details and payment information
  7. Receive confirmation with booking ID
Can I modify my booking? ▼
Yes! You can modify most bookings up to 24 hours before the event. Go to your Dashboard → My Bookings and select the booking you want to modify. Please note that changes may be subject to additional charges or refunds depending on availability.
When will I receive my tickets? ▼
Digital tickets are sent to your registered email immediately upon successful booking confirmation. You'll also receive an SMS with your booking details and a QR code that you can use at the venue.
What is a QR code and how do I use it? ▼
A QR code is a unique barcode for your booking. Simply show this code on your mobile phone or printed ticket to the venue staff for verification. No need to carry physical tickets!

💳 Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:
  • 💳 Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
  • 📱 UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • 💰 Digital Wallets (Paytm, PayPal, etc.)
  • 🏦 Net Banking
Why was my payment declined? ▼
Payment declines can happen due to several reasons:
  • Insufficient funds in your account
  • Incorrect card details
  • Card not enabled for online transactions
  • 3D Secure authentication failed
  • Temporary server issues
Please try again or contact your bank for assistance.
Do you have any discounts or promo codes? ▼
Yes! We regularly offer discounts and promotional codes. You can enter promo codes during checkout to get special offers. Subscribe to our newsletter to receive exclusive promo codes and early access to deals!
Are there any hidden charges? ▼
No! We believe in transparent pricing. The price you see is what you pay. A small convenience fee may apply depending on the payment method, but this is clearly displayed before you complete your booking.

❌ Cancellation & Refunds

What is your cancellation policy? ▼
You can cancel most bookings up to 24 hours before the event:
  • ✅ 24+ hours before: Full refund (minus convenience fees)
  • ⚠️ 12-24 hours before: 75% refund
  • ❌ Less than 12 hours: No refund
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
How do I cancel my booking? ▼
To cancel your booking:
  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Select "My Bookings"
  3. Find the booking you want to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Booking"
  5. Confirm the cancellation
  6. You'll see the refund amount and timeline
When will I receive my refund? ▼
Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after your cancellation is approved. However, it may take an additional 2-3 business days for your bank to reflect the amount in your account, depending on your financial institution.

👤 Account & Profile

How do I reset my password? ▼
If you've forgotten your password:
  1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Use your new password to login
How do I update my profile information? ▼
To update your profile:
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to Dashboard → Profile Settings
  3. Edit your desired information
  4. Click "Save Changes"
You can update your name, phone number, and profile bio.
How do I delete my account? ▼
To permanently delete your account:
  1. Go to Profile Settings
  2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
  3. Click "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion (this action cannot be undone)
⚠️ Warning: Deleting your account will remove all your data permanently.
Is my information secure? ▼
Yes! We take security very seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. Always use a strong password and keep your account credentials safe.
